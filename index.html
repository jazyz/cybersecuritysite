What is Digital Privacy?

Privacy in the physical world is closing your bedroom curtains when getting ready for bed, or locking your doors at night. This seems like second nature to us, but in the digital space the idea of privacy is skewed. We should have just as much privacy online as we have in real life. We should be able to use the internet without constant fear of being tracked.

Why Should we Care?

As our lives become more integrated with technology, the concern about our online personal privacy is rising. Our data is being collected unknowingly or without our consent.
